A web platform for the Institute of Solution Chemistry (ISC RAS), providing scientists with online access to predictive chemical AI models. The service forecasts the properties of molecules, reactions, and mixtures using machine learning.

Acted as a fullstack developer, architecting and building the project from scratch. I developed the client interface on WordPress and a robust Django backend to handle resource-intensive computations and communication with AI models.
