An ED-tech platform for immersive "vibecoding" and applied learning. The service provides an interactive environment that bridges theory with practice in real-world development scenarios.

As the Frontend Lead, I built the communication processes between the design, backend, and frontend teams from scratch, ensuring a unified product vision. I designed the frontend architecture, including a complex domain layer for an online IDE with full file system capabilities. I developed an interactive task system structured into comprehensive learning levels, taking the educational process to a qualitatively new, practice-oriented level.
